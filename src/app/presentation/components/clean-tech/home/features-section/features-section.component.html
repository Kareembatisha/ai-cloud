Stock Management System

The system includes three main pages for managing inventory:

Category Page

Used to manage different item categories such as Soap and Napkins, along with their units of measurement.

Material Page

Displays available materials with details such as quantity, price, and minimum stock level.

Add Material Page

Allows users to add new materials by entering the name, category, quantity, minimum level, and description.

Details Page

Provides an in-depth view of a specific material, including full stock history, price changes, and usage logs.
